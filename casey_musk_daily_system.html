The face
Skincare
Your products are well chosen. The key is order, timing, and consistency. Wrong order = products cancel each other out. Consistency over 3 months is when the results show.
Critical rule — retinol
Retinol is evening only. Never morning. Sunlight breaks it down (makes it useless) and it makes skin sensitive to UV damage. Always the absolute last step at night. Start 3x per week — Mon / Wed / Fri — then build to nightly over 2–3 months as your skin adjusts. Expect some peeling in weeks 1–2. That's normal.
Morning routine
7:00am · 5 steps · 5 minutes
1
Gentle cleanser
Lukewarm water. Don't over-wash — stripping oily skin makes it produce more oil. Gentle circular motion, rinse fully, pat dry.
removes overnight buildup
2
Hyaluronic acid
Apply to damp skin — this is critical. Pat face dry but leave slightly damp. 2–3 drops, press gently. HA pulls moisture from the air — it needs water present to work. Dry skin = wasted product.
hydration — oily skin still needs moisture
3
Peptides
Over the hyaluronic acid. A few drops, press in. Peptides signal skin to produce collagen — your anti-aging investment. Safe morning and evening.
anti-aging · collagen production
4
Multipeptide eye serum
Ring finger only — weakest finger, won't tug the delicate eye area. Tap gently around the orbital bone. Never directly on the eyelid. A little goes a long way.
dark circles · fine lines · anti-aging
5
Oil Control ISDIN
Final step — seals everything in. Check if it contains SPF. If it does, you're done. If not, add a sunscreen on top — sunscreen is the single most important anti-aging product you can use. Non-negotiable.
oil control · seals routine · SPF if applicable
Sauna tip: After your sauna, pores are open and skin is primed. Apply hyaluronic acid immediately post-sauna before anything else — it'll absorb deeper than at any other point in the day. Then finish the rest of the routine at home.
Evening routine
21:00pm · 6 steps · 7 minutes
1
Cleanser
Proper cleanse — remove the day: sweat from gym, pollution from the walks, any buildup. Lukewarm water, gentle circles, rinse fully. Pat dry, don't rub.
remove sweat · pollution · day buildup
2
Hyaluronic acid
Same as morning — apply to damp skin. At night your skin absorbs ingredients better. Sets up the perfect base for everything that follows.
deep overnight hydration
3
Peptides
Over the hyaluronic acid. Night is when skin repairs itself — peptides work hardest while you sleep. Press in gently.
overnight collagen repair
4
Oxy ISDIN eyes
Evening eye treatment. Ring finger, tap gently around the orbital bone. Targets puffiness and dark circles overnight. Use this at night — multipeptide serum in the morning. Don't layer both at once.
depuff · brighten overnight
5
Retinol — Mon / Wed / Fri only
Always last. Pea-sized amount across whole face. Less is more. Avoid the eye area completely — the Oxy ISDIN handles that. Start 3x per week, build to nightly over 2–3 months. Expect some dryness or peeling week 1–2 — that's it working.
anti-aging · cell turnover · acne · the big one
6
Oil Control ISDIN — Tue / Thu / Sat / Sun only
On non-retinol nights, skip step 5 and finish with this instead. Your final moisturising step on the nights your skin gets a retinol break.
hydration on rest nights

Daily laws

Skincare morning and evening. No exceptions.
5 minutes in the morning, 7 at night. Consistency over 3 months is when results show. The routine compounds exactly like the content does.

Never morning. Never near the eyes. Never more than a pea-size. Expect week 1–2 adjustment. Don't stop — that's it working.
5
3 tasks the night before. One music, one content, one income.
Wake up with a mission. Three things only — nothing else gets written down. Clarity is the point.
6
Post every day. Even badly.
A rough 30-second video filmed in 10 minutes beats no video. The algorithm rewards consistency over perfection. So does the audience.
7
All communication lives in the 14:00–16:00 window.
Texts, DMs, client messages, emails — batched here. They don't touch your creative hours. Creative Casey and income Casey stay separate.
8
Announce your deadlines publicly.
"Track drops Friday." "Video out tonight." Say it on stories before it's done. Your word becomes the pressure. Public commitment is the cheapest form of accountability.
9

10
Same structure every day. The freedom is in the music, not the schedule.
The schedule removes decisions. Decisions cost creative energy. Every choice you don't have to make before 8:20am is energy you spend on the thing that actually matters.
